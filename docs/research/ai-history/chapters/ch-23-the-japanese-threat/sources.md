## Citation Bar

- Fuchi 1984 pp.18-23 for original philosophy, kernel language, non-von-Neumann inference machine, and "not an AI/expert-system project" guardrail.
- Kinoshita 1984 pp.7-12 for Japanese computerization and software-crisis context.
- Furukawa 1987 pp.1-3 for project rationale and the logic-programming bridge.
- Kurozumi 1992 pp.9-13 for launch, stages, budget, and PSI/PIM progression.

- FinalEval p.4, Gallaire 1992 p.64, and Uchida 1994 section 1 for mixed evaluation and dissemination.

## Source Discipline Notes

  OCR with `pdftoppm` plus `tesseract`. Verify any direct quote against the page
  image before final prose.
- Kurozumi 1992 and Fuchi 1992 are text-extractable from the Bitsavers-hosted
  ICOT proceedings. Page anchors use printed proceedings page numbers.
- The OTA PDF is text-extractable; page anchors use the printed OTA chapter page
  numbers.
- The NTRL Strategic Computing record is metadata/abstract only. Do not turn it
  into detailed DARPA prose until the plan PDF is retrieved and parsed or a
  reliable full-text source is added.
- Wikipedia was used for source discovery only. Do not cite it in prose.
