# People: Chapter 45 - Generative Adversarial Networks

## Core 2014 GAN Authors

- **Ian J. Goodfellow:** First author of "Generative Adversarial Nets." The paper footnote says he was a Google research scientist by publication but did the work earlier as a University of Montreal student. Use him as the origin-story protagonist, but credit the full author group for the paper.
- **Jean Pouget-Abadie:** Co-author; paper footnote says he did the work while visiting Universite de Montreal from Ecole Polytechnique.
- **Mehdi Mirza:** Co-author in the University of Montreal group; relevant mainly as part of the original formulation team.
- **Bing Xu:** Co-author in the University of Montreal group; relevant mainly as part of the original formulation team.
- **David Warde-Farley:** Co-author in the University of Montreal group; also appears in Theano/Pylearn2 ecosystem references.
- **Sherjil Ozair:** Co-author; paper footnote says he was visiting Universite de Montreal from IIT Delhi.
- **Aaron Courville:** Co-author and Montreal deep learning researcher; relevant as part of the original team.
- **Yoshua Bengio:** Co-author; paper footnote identifies him as a CIFAR Senior Fellow. Important for the Montreal lab context but should not be made the scene's protagonist unless separately sourced.

## Prior-Work / Boundary Figure

- **Jurgen Schmidhuber:** Author of predictability minimization work. The 2014 GAN paper names predictability minimization as the most relevant prior work with competing neural networks and then distinguishes the GAN formulation from it. Use for conflict discipline, not as a digression.

## DCGAN Bridge

- **Alec Radford:** DCGAN first author; contributed the architecture-constrained convolutional GAN bridge and latent-vector arithmetic demonstrations.
- **Luke Metz:** DCGAN co-author at indico Research.
- **Soumith Chintala:** DCGAN co-author at Facebook AI Research; the paper thanks NVIDIA for a Titan-X GPU used in the work.

## NVIDIA High-Resolution Arc

- **Tero Karras:** Lead author on Progressive GAN and StyleGAN; NVIDIA Research.
- **Timo Aila:** Co-author on Progressive GAN and StyleGAN; NVIDIA Research.
- **Samuli Laine:** Co-author on Progressive GAN and StyleGAN; NVIDIA Research.
- **Jaakko Lehtinen:** Progressive GAN co-author; NVIDIA and Aalto University. The StyleGAN README thanks him for discussions and comments even though he is not a StyleGAN paper co-author.

## Secondary-Source Voices

- **Martin Giles:** MIT Technology Review journalist whose 2018 profile supplies the Les 3 Brasseurs narrative. Treat as a secondary source, not a primary witness transcript.
- **Yann LeCun, Andrew Ng, Pedro Domingos, Hany Farid:** Appear in the MIT Technology Review profile as external commentators. Useful only for a short reception/risk paragraph if the prose has room.
