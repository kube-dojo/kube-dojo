## Conflict Notes

- Do not say ChatGPT invented RLHF, Transformers, scaling laws, or language models.
- Do not say ChatGPT still holds the fastest-consumer-app record. Say Reuters/UBS estimated it was the fastest-growing consumer application at the time.
- Do not overuse "panic" or "code red" unless citing a secondary report; official Google/Microsoft posts are enough for the product-race layer.
- Do not move RAG/plugins/tool-use into this chapter beyond a transition sentence; Ch60 owns that.
- Do not make the education section a moral panic chapter. Use it to show institutional stress from fluent, cheap, uncertain text.
- Do not treat ChatGPT's limitations as solved by GPT-4. OpenAI's GPT-4 page still says hallucinations and risks remain.
