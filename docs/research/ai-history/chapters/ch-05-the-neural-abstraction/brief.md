# Brief: Chapter 5 - The Neural Abstraction

## Thesis
The fundamental architecture of deep learning began with a physiological revelation: the messy, biological human brain could be abstracted into a network of clean, binary logic gates. McCulloch and Pitts proved that biological intelligence and artificial computation shared the same underlying mathematics.

## Scope
- IN SCOPE: Warren McCulloch, Walter Pitts, the 1943 paper "A Logical Calculus of the Ideas Immanent in Nervous Activity".
- OUT OF SCOPE: The Perceptron (1958).

## Scenes Outline
1. **The Neurophysiologist and the Prodigy:** The meeting of Warren McCulloch and the brilliant, eccentric Walter Pitts.
2. **The Biological Switch:** The realization that a neuron either fires or it doesn't (all-or-nothing, 1 or 0).
3. **The Logical Calculus:** The 1943 proof that networks of idealized artificial neurons can compute any logical proposition.
