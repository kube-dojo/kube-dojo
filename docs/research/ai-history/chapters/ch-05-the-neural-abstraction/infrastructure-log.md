# Infrastructure Log: Chapter 5

## Technical Metrics & Constraints
- **The Idealized Neuron:**
  - Architecture: Binary threshold units. They sum inputs, and if the sum crosses a threshold, they fire (output 1).
  - Constraint: The 1943 model was hard-coded; it lacked a mechanism for *learning* or adjusting its own weights over time (which Rosenblatt would later add).
