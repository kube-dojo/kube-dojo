## Conflict Notes
- **Minsky's Intent:** Minsky later claimed he didn't intend to kill neural network funding entirely, just to redirect it to more fruitful avenues. The chapter will focus on the *infrastructural consequence*: without the compute to simulate deep networks, DARPA and ONR looked at the mathematical proof and decided connectionism wasn't worth the hardware investment.
