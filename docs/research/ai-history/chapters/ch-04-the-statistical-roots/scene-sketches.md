# Scene Sketches: Chapter 4

## Scene 1: The Dispute
- **Setting:** St. Petersburg, Russia (1913).
- **Action:** Markov sets out to disprove the theological arguments of his rival Nekrasov regarding free will and independent events.

## Scene 2: Counting Pushkin
- **Action:** Pedagogical explanation of a Markov Chain. If the current letter is a consonant, what is the mathematical probability the next is a vowel?

## Scene 3: The Predictive Seed
- **Action:** The realization that text generation doesn't need to "understand" grammar; it just needs to know the statistical likelihood of the next token.
