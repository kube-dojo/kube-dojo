# Scene Sketches: Chapter 44

## Scene 1: The One-Hot Problem
- **Action:** A computer treating "dog" and "cat" as completely unrelated integers, unable to recognize their shared "pet-ness."

## Scene 2: The Embedding
- **Action:** Pedagogical explanation of continuous vectors. Words are placed in a 300-dimensional space where words that appear in similar contexts are clustered together.

## Scene 3: The King and Queen
- **Action:** The magical moment when researchers subtract the vector for "man" from "king" and add "woman," landing exactly on the vector for "queen."
