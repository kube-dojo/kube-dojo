# People: Chapter 38 - The Human API

## Amazon and MTurk

- **Venky Harinarayan** - Listed inventor on US7197459B1 with Anand Rajaraman and Anand Ranganathan. IEEE Spectrum identifies him as the Amazon manager whose patent described the hybrid machine/human computing arrangement. Verified anchors: US7197459B1 Info lines 21-28; IEEE Spectrum lines 101-105.
- **Anand Rajaraman** - Listed inventor on US7197459B1. Verified anchor: US7197459B1 Info lines 21-28.
- **Anand Ranganathan** - Listed inventor on US7197459B1. Verified anchor: US7197459B1 Info lines 21-28.
- **Jeff Bezos** - Amazon founder/CEO. Computerworld reports his September 27, 2006 MIT keynote framing AWS as the "hidden Amazon" and highlighting MTurk, S3, and EC2. Pew attributes the MTurk website idea to Bezos and quotes the NYT/Pontin account, but the single-originator claim remains Yellow because it needs reconciliation with the patent/inventor story. Verified anchors: Computerworld lines 228-242; Pew lines 147-148.
- **Jeff Barr** - AWS Chief Evangelist and authorial presence on later AWS blog posts; not necessary for the main chapter unless the prose uses AWS News Blog continuity. No load-bearing Green claim currently depends on him.

## Research Adopters

- **Rion Snow** - Co-author of the 2008 EMNLP paper validating MTurk for NLP annotation. Verified anchor: ACL Anthology metadata lines 8-10 and Snow et al. PDF p.254.
- **Brendan O'Connor** - Co-author of Snow et al. 2008; affiliated with Dolores Labs in the paper. Verified anchor: Snow et al. PDF p.254 lines 3-17.
- **Daniel Jurafsky** - Co-author of Snow et al. 2008; Stanford Linguistics. Verified anchor: Snow et al. PDF p.254 lines 3-17.
- **Andrew Y. Ng** - Co-author of Snow et al. 2008; Stanford Computer Science. Verified anchor: Snow et al. PDF p.254 lines 3-17.
- **Jia Deng, Wei Dong, Richard Socher, Li-Jia Li, Kai Li, and Li Fei-Fei** - Authors of the 2009 ImageNet CVPR paper. Their chapter role is the ImageNet-scale application of MTurk-based cleaning, not the later ImageNet competition/AlexNet story. Verified anchor: ImageNet PDF p.248 lines 0-3.

## Boundary and Later Measurement

- **Luis von Ahn and Laura Dabbish** - Authors of the 2004 ESP Game paper. Their role is boundary-setting: MTurk did not invent human computation or image labeling. Exact page anchors still Yellow for this contract.
- **Kotaro Hara, Abigail Adams, Kristy Milland, Saiph Savage, Chris Callison-Burch, and Jeffrey P. Bigham** - Authors of the 2018 CHI wage analysis. Their role is the closing boundary on hidden labor and low effective wages. Verified anchor: Hara et al. PDF p.1 lines 0-24.

## People Not to Overuse

- **Jason Pontin** - Author of the 2007 New York Times article often cited for Bezos's "artificial artificial intelligence" explanation. The article text was blocked here, so he remains a citation-bar target, not a source for direct quotation.
- **Anonymous MTurk workers** - Central to the chapter's moral and infrastructural point, but do not invent individual worker scenes. Use aggregate studies unless an independently sourced worker account is added later.
