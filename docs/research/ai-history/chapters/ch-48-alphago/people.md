# People: Chapter 48 - AlphaGo

## DeepMind And Google

- **David Silver** - Lead AlphaGo researcher and co-corresponding author of the *Nature* paper. WIRED interviewed him after Move 37 about AlphaGo's low human-move probability estimate. Anchors: `sources.md` S1 p.484 author list; S1 Author Contributions G25; S5/G27.
- **Aja Huang** - AlphaGo co-author; British Go Association reports that he placed AlphaGo's moves on the physical board during the Lee Sedol match. Anchors: `sources.md` G25, G28.
- **Demis Hassabis** - DeepMind CEO/co-founder, co-author and co-corresponding author of the *Nature* paper; author of Google's January and March 2016 public AlphaGo posts. Anchors: `sources.md` S1, S2, S3.
- **Thore Graepel, Arthur Guez, Chris Maddison, Laurent Sifre, George van den Driessche, Julian Schrittwieser, Ioannis Antonoglou, Veda Panneershelvam, Marc Lanctot, Sander Dieleman, Dominik Grewe, John Nham, Nal Kalchbrenner, Ilya Sutskever, Timothy Lillicrap, Madeleine Leach, Koray Kavukcuoglu** - AlphaGo paper co-authors. The contribution statement divides work across search, neural-network training, evaluation, management, advising, and writing. Anchor: `sources.md` G25.

## Go Players And Commentators

- **Lee Sedol** - Korean 9-dan professional Go player; Google's public framing calls him the best Go player of the prior decade. In the match he lost Games 1-3, won Game 4, and lost Game 5 for a 4-1 final score. Anchors: `sources.md` G15-G21. Title-count claims such as "18-time world champion" remain Yellow (`sources.md` Y3).
- **Fan Hui** - Reigning three-time European Go champion in Google's January 2016 account; lost to AlphaGo 5-0 in a closed-door October 2015 match. Later reacted strongly to Move 37 in WIRED's on-site reporting. Anchors: `sources.md` G13-G14, G26.
- **Michael Redmond** - 9-dan professional commentator for the English-language DeepMind/Google match coverage; quoted in Google's match log and British Go Association report. Anchors: `sources.md` G18, G20-G21, G28.
- **Chris Garlock** - American Go E-Journal editor/commentator for the English match coverage; quoted in Google's match log and named in BGA report. Anchor: `sources.md` S3, S7.
- **Yoo Changhyuk, Kim Seongryong, Song Taegon, Lee Hyunwook** - Korean professional commentators quoted in Google's game summaries. Anchors: `sources.md` S3 Game 2-5 sections.
