## Source Discipline Notes

- Wikipedia may be used only for discovery and date sanity checks, not as a
  prose claim anchor.
- Van Vleck/Multicians is valuable as a participant guide and pointer to primary
  reports, but claims should be upgraded to Green only when backed by the
  reports, Fano, MIT, DARPA, or another primary/credible institutional source.
- Do not use unsourced budget figures from retrospective pages as Green unless
  they are backed by a primary report or cited historical source.
- Use Norberg/O'Neill for ARPA/IPTO strategy, not for technical details already
  better anchored in the progress reports.
- Avoid long verbatim passages from Fano or progress reports; paraphrase.
