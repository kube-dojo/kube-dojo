# Scene Sketches: Chapter 36

## Scene 1: The Speedometer
- **Action:** Consumers buying PCs based purely on the GHz number on the box.

## Scene 2: The Thermal Wall
- **Action:** Pedagogical explanation of Dennard scaling. Why shrinking transistors eventually leads to current leakage and unmanageable heat.

## Scene 3: The Free Lunch is Over
- **Action:** Software engineers realize their old, single-threaded code won't run any faster on next year's hardware. They must embrace parallelism, setting the stage for massive matrix math.
