## Infrastructure Cautions

- Do not present any single machine as "the computer that created AI." The chapter's point is the funded network of machines, languages, people, and sites.
- Do not make Project MAC's full infrastructure arc the center of Ch16. Use enough to explain the funding mechanism and hand off to Ch20.
- Do not state exact AI-center contract totals unless a contract or budget exhibit is in hand. The Green anchors support general scale and selected figures, not a full ledger.
