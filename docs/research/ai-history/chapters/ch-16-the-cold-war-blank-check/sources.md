## Conflict Notes

- Treat `DARPA` as modern institutional name and `ARPA` as period name until 1972.
- Project MAC figures vary by source and year. Use the $3-million Project MAC contract only where anchored to Norberg/O'Neill; avoid turning that into "MIT AI got $3 million."
- NRC says Project MAC allocated roughly one-third of its $2.3 million annual budget to AI research; Norberg/O'Neill separately reports the $3-million Project MAC contract. Keep those claims distinct.
- Edwards' "AI piggybacked" interpretation is useful but interpretive. Attribute if used in prose.
