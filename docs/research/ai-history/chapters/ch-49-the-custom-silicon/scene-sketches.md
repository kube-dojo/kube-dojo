# Scene Sketches: Chapter 49

## Scene 1: The Voice Search Threat
- **Action:** Deep learning significantly improves speech recognition, but running the neural network on CPUs for every Android user is economically impossible.

## Scene 2: The Heartbeat of Math
- **Action:** Pedagogical explanation of a systolic array. Instead of constantly reading/writing to RAM (the von Neumann bottleneck), data pumps through a massive physical grid of multipliers.

## Scene 3: The Custom Moat
- **Action:** Google hides the TPUs in their racks for years before announcing them, sparking an industry-wide race for custom AI silicon (AWS Trainium, etc.).
