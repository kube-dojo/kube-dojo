# Scene Sketches: Chapter 3

## Scene 1: The Mess of Wires
- **Setting:** MIT (1937).
- **Action:** Engineers struggling to design complex circuits by intuition.

## Scene 2: The Insight
- **Action:** Shannon realizes an open switch is 0 (False) and a closed switch is 1 (True). Series circuits are AND gates; parallel circuits are OR gates.

## Scene 3: The Paradigm Shift
- **Action:** The physical machine is no longer just moving parts; it is a manifestation of pure mathematical logic.
