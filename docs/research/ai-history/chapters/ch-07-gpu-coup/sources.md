## Conflict Notes
- **Hinton's vs. Krizhevsky's Credit:** While Geoffrey Hinton was the visionary supervisor, the narrative will strictly follow the infrastructure angle: Alex Krizhevsky was the one writing the raw CUDA kernels and managing the physical GTX 580s. The chapter will focus on Krizhevsky's engineering feats as the critical enabler of Hinton's theories.
