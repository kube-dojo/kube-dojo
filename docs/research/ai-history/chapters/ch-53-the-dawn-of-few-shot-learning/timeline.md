# Timeline: Chapter 53 - The Dawn of Few-Shot Learning

- **2018:** OpenAI's first GPT work establishes Transformer language-model pre-training as a reusable NLP base; GPT-2 paper later treats the smallest GPT-2-size model as equivalent to original GPT.
- **February 2019:** OpenAI publishes the GPT-2 paper and releases the 124M-parameter model while withholding larger models and WebText data.
- **May 2019:** OpenAI releases the 355M-parameter GPT-2 model, output datasets from all four model sizes, and detection-related material.
- **August 2019:** OpenAI releases the 774M-parameter GPT-2 model and a first version of the release-strategy/social-impact report.
- **November 2019:** OpenAI releases the 1.5B-parameter GPT-2 model and updates the report and GitHub documentation.
- **May 2020:** OpenAI publishes the GPT-3 paper on arXiv, reporting a 175B-parameter autoregressive model evaluated in zero-shot, one-shot, and few-shot settings without gradient updates.
