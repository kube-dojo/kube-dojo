# Scene Sketches: Chapter 47

## Scene 1: The Degradation Mystery
- **Action:** Adding layers to a network mathematically shouldn't increase training error, but in practice, it does. Gradients are vanishing.

## Scene 2: The Shortcut
- **Action:** Pedagogical explanation of residual blocks. Instead of forcing a layer to learn a complex transformation, give it the original input via a shortcut, and only ask it to learn the *difference* (the residual).

## Scene 3: Unlocking Depth
- **Action:** The 2015 ImageNet victory. ResNet achieves human-level error rates (~3.5%) on the classification task.
