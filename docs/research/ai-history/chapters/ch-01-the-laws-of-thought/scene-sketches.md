# Scene Sketches: Chapter 1

## Scene 1: The Mathematician's Obsession
- **Setting:** Queen's College, Cork, Ireland (1854).
- **Action:** Boole observes the messy nature of human arguments and seeks a mathematical purity.

## Scene 2: The Binary Reduction
- **Action:** Pedagogical explanation of Boole's insight: replacing words with symbols (x, y) and logic with math (+, *), reducing the universe to 1 and 0.

## Scene 3: The Dormant Blueprint
- **Action:** Boole dies unaware that his abstract algebra will one day become the foundational instruction set for all physical computing infrastructure.
