# People: Chapter 1

## Verified Protagonists
- **George Boole (1815–1864):** Self-taught English mathematician. Invented Boolean algebra in 1854, laying the mathematical (but not physical) foundation for binary computing.
- **Mary Everest Boole (1832–1916):** Mathematician and educator who played a crucial role in preserving and disseminating George Boole's work after his death.
