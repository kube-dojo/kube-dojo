# Brief: Chapter 6 - The Cybernetics Movement

## Thesis
Before AI focused on disembodied software, it was deeply obsessed with physical interaction. The Cybernetics movement, led by Norbert Wiener, viewed intelligence entirely as a physical system of homeostasis and negative feedback loops, setting the analog baseline that digital AI would eventually rebel against.

## Scope
- IN SCOPE: Norbert Wiener, the Macy Conferences, homeostasis, negative feedback loops.
- OUT OF SCOPE: Grey Walter's Tortoises (next chapter).

## Scenes Outline
1. **The Anti-Aircraft Gun:** Wiener's WWII work on predicting bomber trajectories via feedback loops.
2. **The Macy Conferences:** Interdisciplinary debates in New York defining intelligence as control and communication.
3. **The Philosophy of Homeostasis:** Viewing machines and biological organisms as functionally identical systems reacting to the environment.
