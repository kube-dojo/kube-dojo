# Scene Sketches: Chapter 6

## Scene 1: Tracking the Target
- **Setting:** WWII research labs.
- **Action:** Wiener realizes that correcting error in a mechanical gun turret is the same process as a human reaching for a glass of water.

## Scene 2: The Beekman Hotel Debates
- **Action:** The Macy Conferences. Diverse scientists argue that if a machine adapts like an animal, it is "alive" in the ways that matter.

## Scene 3: The Continuous World
- **Action:** Pedagogical demonstration of negative feedback (like a thermostat) versus discrete digital logic.
