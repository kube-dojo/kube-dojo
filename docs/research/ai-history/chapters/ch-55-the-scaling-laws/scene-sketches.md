# Scene Sketches: Chapter 55

## Scene 1: The Bitter Lesson
- **Action:** Sutton observes that researchers keep trying to build human knowledge into AI, but simply scaling up search and learning always wins.

## Scene 2: Plotting the Curve
- **Action:** The OpenAI team runs models of various sizes and realizes the graph of their performance forms a perfect, straight line on a log-log plot.

## Scene 3: The Industrial Era
- **Action:** The realization that to reach human-level AI, they just need to extend the line, which requires billions of dollars of data center infrastructure.
