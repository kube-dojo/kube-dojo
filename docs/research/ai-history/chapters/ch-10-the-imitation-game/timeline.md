## Notes on dating

- The chapter's load-bearing dates are 20 February 1947, the 1948 composition of *Intelligent Machinery*, and October 1950 (the *Mind* publication). All three are Green-anchored.
- The 1952 BBC dates (recording and broadcast) are Yellow because the broadcast text itself is not directly verified, even though the dates are widely standard. The chapter can use the dates without overclaiming the content.
- The 1948 NPL report's *publication* date (1968) is a different matter from its *composition* date (1948); the chapter must not blur the two.
- No "founding date of AI" appears in this timeline. That is Ch11's territory. The chapter's stance is that 1950 was a *protocol* moment, not a *founding* moment — see `brief.md` Boundary Contract.
