# Scene Sketches: Chapter 10

## Scene 1: The Parlor Game
- **Setting:** Manchester (1950).
- **Action:** Turing adapts a Victorian party game involving a man and a woman into a test between a human and a machine.

## Scene 2: The Text Interface
- **Action:** Turing explicitly removes physical robotics from the equation. Intelligence is defined purely by text generation.

## Scene 3: The Defense
- **Action:** Turing systematically dismantles the arguments that machines cannot be creative or make mistakes, defining the intellectual roadmap for the next 70 years of AI.
