# Timeline: Chapter 6

- **2005:** Amazon launches Mechanical Turk as a crowdsourcing internet marketplace.
- **2006:** Fei-Fei Li, frustrated by the limitations of datasets like PASCAL VOC, begins conceptualizing a massive visual dataset mapped to the WordNet hierarchy.
- **2007:** The project pivots from using undergraduates to using Amazon Mechanical Turk, unlocking the necessary scale.
- **June 2009:** The ImageNet paper is presented as a poster at the CVPR conference in Miami.
- **2010:** The first ImageNet Large Scale Visual Recognition Challenge (ILSVRC) is held.
