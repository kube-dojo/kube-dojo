# Scene Sketches: Chapter 50

## Scene 1: The Memory Problem
- **Action:** LSTMs forget the beginning of a long paragraph by the time they reach the end because they process sequentially.

## Scene 2: The Brainstorm
- **Action:** The team at Google realizes that if you just look at all words at once and calculate their relevance to each other, you don't need to read left-to-right.

## Scene 3: The Parallel Exploit
- **Action:** Because the operations are just massive matrix multiplications without sequential dependencies, the algorithm maps perfectly to the physics of GPUs.
