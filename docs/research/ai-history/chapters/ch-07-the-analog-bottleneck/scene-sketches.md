# Scene Sketches: Chapter 7

## Scene 1: The Dance of the Tortoises
- **Setting:** Bristol, England (1950).
- **Action:** Observers watch the machines avoid obstacles and seek light, assuming they possess intent.

## Scene 2: The Plumber's Dilemma
- **Action:** Opening the plastic shell. Showing the messy reality of the wiring. The hardware *is* the behavior.

## Scene 3: The Wall of Heat
- **Action:** Calculating what a "brain" made of vacuum tubes would require: a skyscraper, a city's power grid, and constant replacing of burnt-out glass.
