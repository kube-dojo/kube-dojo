# People: Chapter 29 - Support Vector Machines

- **Vladimir Vapnik:** Central figure in VC theory, statistical learning theory, optimal hyperplanes, and support-vector networks; coauthor of both Boser/Guyon/Vapnik 1992 and Cortes/Vapnik 1995.
- **Alexey Chervonenkis:** Vapnik's collaborator in the uniform-convergence/VC theory lineage.
- **Bernhard Boser:** Coauthor of the 1992 optimal-margin training algorithm.
- **Isabelle Guyon:** Coauthor of the 1992 optimal-margin training algorithm; also part of the Bell Labs OCR/SVM ecosystem.
- **Corinna Cortes:** Coauthor of the 1995 support-vector networks paper; central to soft-margin SVMs and OCR benchmark experiments.
- **Christopher Burges:** Author of the 1998 SVM tutorial that helped transmit the method to the pattern-recognition/data-mining community.
- **Yann LeCun and Leon Bottou:** Contextual Bell Labs figures for OCR benchmarks and comparison against LeNet/statistical classifiers.
