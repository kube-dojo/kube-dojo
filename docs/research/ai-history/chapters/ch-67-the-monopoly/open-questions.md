# Open Questions

## Resolved For Drafting

- **Is this chapter claiming a legal monopoly?** No. The title is narrative; the
  contract uses concentration, bottleneck, chokepoint, and platform power unless
  a legal finding is added later.
- **Can the chapter say Microsoft/OpenAI exclusivity still exists?** Only with
  dates. 2019, Oct. 2025, Feb. 2026, and Apr. 27, 2026 sources describe different
  terms. The latest sourced state here is Apr. 27, 2026: Microsoft remains primary
  cloud partner, OpenAI can serve products across any cloud, and Microsoft's
  license is non-exclusive through 2032.
- **Should NVIDIA be treated as antitrust finding?** No. Use NVIDIA's 10-K for
  demand and revenue facts; use regulator statements for bottleneck-risk framing.
- **Are cloud partnerships neutral or harmful?** The contract does not decide.
  Company sources describe partnership benefits; regulator sources describe risks
  and information gaps.
