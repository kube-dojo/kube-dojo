## Conflict Notes

- Microsoft/OpenAI exclusivity changed over time. Use exact dates: 2019
  exclusive cloud provider; Oct. 2025 revised rights; Feb. 2026 clarification;
  Apr. 27, 2026 primary-cloud/non-exclusive license amendment.
- Company partnership announcements are evidence of what companies said and did,
  not neutral analysis of market harm.
- Regulator sources describe risks, inquiries, and studies. Do not turn them into
  legal findings.
