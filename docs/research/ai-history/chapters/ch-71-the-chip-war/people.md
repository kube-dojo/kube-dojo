## Narrative Handling

The protagonists are institutions and bottlenecks more than individuals. Human
texture can come from roles: export-control lawyers, procurement teams, fab
planners, packaging engineers, and sales teams rewriting product lines around
moving thresholds. Do not invent private dialogue.
