## Conflict Notes

- The chapter should distinguish U.S. government rationale from observed market
  outcome. BIS says controls target military modernization; prose should not
  independently assert all Chinese AI work is military.
- H20 is an example of product-threshold adaptation. Do not let it become a
  detailed NVIDIA business chapter.
- TSMC/ASML company reports are self-descriptions. Use them for factual
  operations/capacity statements, not neutral judgments of strategic virtue.
- The AI Diffusion rule was rescinded; do not write as if it is active policy.
- The allied-coordination scene should not claim a public treaty. Anchor it in
  visible Dutch/Japanese national measures and their functional alignment with
  U.S. controls.
- CSET/MOFCOM translation is strong enough for the counter-lever scene, but if
  prose needs exact Chinese legal wording, ask for a Chinese-language check.
