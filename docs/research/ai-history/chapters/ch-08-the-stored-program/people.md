# People: Chapter 8

## Verified Protagonists
- **John von Neumann (1903–1957):** Authored the EDVAC report.
- **J. Presper Eckert (1919–1995) & John Mauchly (1907–1980):** Chief engineers of ENIAC and originators of many stored-program concepts.
- **Herman Goldstine (1913–2004):** Army liaison who circulated von Neumann's draft.
- **The ENIAC Programmers:** e.g., Jean Bartik, Betty Holberton, who physically programmed the machine.
