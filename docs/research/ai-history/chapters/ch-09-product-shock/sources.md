## Conflict Notes
- **AGI vs. Product:** While OpenAI's stated goal is AGI, this chapter strictly focuses on the *productization* infrastructure. RLHF will be framed not as an intelligence breakthrough, but as an interface breakthrough—a way to harness raw compute into a usable software product.
