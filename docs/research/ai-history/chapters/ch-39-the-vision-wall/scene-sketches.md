# Scene Sketches: Chapter 39

## Scene 1: Hand-Tuning the Edge
- **Action:** A researcher spends months mathematically defining what a "corner" or an "edge" looks like (SIFT features), hoping the computer can use it to find a car.

## Scene 2: The Benchmark Plateau
- **Action:** The annual PASCAL VOC conference. Everyone is using Support Vector Machines and hand-crafted features. Progress has slowed to a crawl.

## Scene 3: The Data realization
- **Action:** A few renegade researchers realize the math isn't the problem. The algorithm is starving for variance.
