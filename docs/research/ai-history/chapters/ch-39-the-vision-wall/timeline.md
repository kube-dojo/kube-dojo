# Timeline: Chapter 39 - The Vision Wall

- **1999:** Lowe publishes "Object Recognition from Local Scale-Invariant Features" at ICCV, pp. 1150-1157. Anchor: Lowe 2004 Springer references list, sources.md G01 note.
- **2004:** Lowe's IJCV article "Distinctive Image Features from Scale-Invariant Keypoints" appears in volume 60, pp. 91-110. Anchor: sources.md G01-G02.
- **June 2005:** Dalal and Triggs present "Histograms of Oriented Gradients for Human Detection" at CVPR 2005, pp. 886-893. Anchor: sources.md G03.
- **2005:** PASCAL VOC begins with four classes and 1,578 train/validation/test images containing 2,209 annotated objects. Anchor: VOC2012 History and Background, sources.md G23 context.
- **2007:** VOC establishes the fixed 20-class regime; the 2007 dataset contains 9,963 images and 24,640 annotated objects. Anchor: sources.md G07.
- **2008:** VOC stops releasing full test annotations publicly, moving the benchmark toward hidden-test evaluation. Anchor: sources.md G10.
- **2009:** VOC begins augmenting previous years' images rather than issuing an entirely new classification/detection dataset each year. Anchor: VOC2012 History and Background lines 255-260, sources.md G09 context.
- **2010:** VOC train/val reaches 10,103 images and 23,374 ROI annotated objects; an associated large-scale classification challenge based on ImageNet is introduced. Anchor: sources.md G08.
- **2011:** Torralba and Efros publish "Unbiased Look at Dataset Bias," measuring dataset signatures, cross-dataset generalization, negative-set bias, and sample value. Anchor: sources.md G13-G22.
- **2011:** VOC submissions require a method abstract of at least 500 characters, adding another layer of evaluation-server documentation. Anchor: sources.md G29.
- **2011-2012:** VOC classification/detection trainval data contains 11,530 images and 27,450 ROI annotated objects; VOC2012 reuses VOC2011 classification/detection data. Anchor: sources.md G09.
- **October 12, 2012:** VOC2012 workshop is held with ECCV 2012 and dedicated to Mark Everingham's memory. Anchor: sources.md G25.
- **2015:** Everingham et al.'s retrospective appears in IJCV 111(1), pp. 98-136, reviewing VOC 2008-2012. Anchor: sources.md G23-G24.
