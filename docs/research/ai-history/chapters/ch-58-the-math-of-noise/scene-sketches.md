# Scene Sketches: Chapter 58

## Scene 1: The Drop of Ink
- **Action:** Pedagogical explanation of entropy. A drop of ink in water diffuses into a uniform grey. Reversing time to get the drop back is physically impossible, but mathematically computable.

## Scene 2: The Static
- **Action:** A neural network watches a picture of a cat slowly dissolve into television static.

## Scene 3: Sculpting from Noise
- **Action:** The model is handed pure static and asked to reverse the process. Step by step, it removes noise, and a photorealistic, entirely new cat emerges.
