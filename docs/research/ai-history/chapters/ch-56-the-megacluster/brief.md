# Brief: Chapter 56 - The Megacluster

## Thesis
With the Scaling Laws mathematically proven, the pursuit of AGI transitioned from academic labs to massive industrial logistics. OpenAI partnered with Microsoft to build an unprecedented, dedicated supercomputer in Azure, proving that the future of AI required sovereign-level capital expenditure.

## Scope
- IN SCOPE: OpenAI/Microsoft partnership (2019), the physical engineering of the Azure AI supercomputer, InfiniBand networking, the sheer cost of scale.
- OUT OF SCOPE: Sovereign AI (Part 9).

## Scenes Outline
1. **The Capital Requirement:** OpenAI realizes that to train GPT-3 and beyond, their non-profit structure cannot raise the necessary billions for compute.
2. **The Partnership:** Satya Nadella and Sam Altman forge a historic deal; Microsoft invests $1 billion, largely in the form of Azure compute credits.
3. **Wiring the Beast:** Microsoft engineers physically design and build a custom supercomputer containing tens of thousands of GPUs, connected by specialized high-speed networks to prevent data bottlenecks.
