# Scene Sketches: Chapter 2

## Scene 1: The Unsolvable Problem
- **Setting:** Bologna, Germany (1928).
- **Action:** Hilbert poses the decision problem, unintentionally kicking off the search for a universal computer.

## Scene 2: The Paper Tape
- **Action:** Pedagogical explanation of how a Turing Machine works. It reads a symbol, changes state, writes a symbol, and moves left or right.

## Scene 3: The Birth of Software
- **Action:** Turing realizes that the table of instructions can itself be encoded on the tape. Data and instructions are mathematically the same.
