# Scene Sketches: Chapter 18 - The Lighthill Devastation

## Scene 1: The Commissioned Outsider

Open inside the funding apparatus, not in a laboratory. SRC is receiving rising
AI grant applications across mathematical, engineering, and biological domains.
Lighthill presents himself as a lay outsider who spent two months reading and
consulting. This lets the prose show why one report could matter: it was written
for people who had to decide whether AI was one field, several fields, or a
confusing label attached to many grant requests.

## Scene 2: The ABC Trap

Explain A, B, and C slowly enough that the reader sees the trap form. A has
application fields. C has psychology and neurobiology. B is supposed to bridge
them, but if the bridge looks like "Building Robots" with vague aims, then basic
AI becomes the easiest part to distrust. This is the chapter's policy geometry.

## Scene 3: The Explosion Becomes Administrative

Use Lighthill's examples as a progression: machine translation, speech
recognition, theorem proving, chess, robotics, Winograd's blocks world. The
point is not that nothing worked; the point is that systems worked only in
small, carefully structured domains. The mathematical phrase "combinatorial
explosion" becomes a reason to fund applied islands and distrust general AI.

## Scene 4: The Rebuttal Table

Stage the responses as a live disagreement, not a chorus. Sutherland says B is
Basic research with its own scientific aims. Needham partly agrees with
Lighthill and doubts some of Sutherland's spin-off defense. Michie reframes the
field as Intelligence Theory and complains that leading US figures were not
consulted for opinions. The reader should feel a community fighting over its own
name.

## Scene 5: The Imported Machine

Center Michie's DEC System 10/PDP-10 passage. This turns the controversy from
abstract prestige into infrastructure. UK AI does not merely need permission to
think; it needs machines, languages, operating systems, and compatibility with
the American software world. Funding skepticism therefore compounds technical
dependency.

## Scene 6: Winter Without Extinction

Close by avoiding melodrama. Chilton says the report provoked a UK loss of
confidence lasting almost a decade; House of Lords says the report is mostly
known for reduced UK support but funding data are scant; AI continued and
returned in major policy form under IKBS/Alvey. The devastation is a change in
legitimacy and framing, not a total stop.

The BBC debate may be mentioned as a public flashpoint anchored by the AIAI
listing, but do not paraphrase what any participant said unless the video is
watched and logged.
