# Infrastructure Log: Chapter 18 - The Lighthill Devastation

## Funding / Institutions

- The Science Research Council is the chapter's institutional center. Lighthill
  wrote for a body receiving rising AI grant applications and trying to form a
  general view across Engineering Board and Science Board concerns.
- The report's effect should be framed as loss of confidence and a colder UK
  funding climate, not as a precisely quantified budget collapse. House of Lords
  2018 explicitly notes that systematic IT R&D and AI-specific funding data were
  scant.
- A/C versus B is also an institutional routing problem: category A points toward
  automation, control engineering, industry, and application fields; category C
  points toward psychology and neurobiology; category B lacks an obvious
  external patron unless it can justify itself as Basic AI.

## Compute / Software

- Lighthill repeatedly treats limited universes of discourse as a practical
  boundary: chessboards, puzzles, and table-top block worlds let programs look
  more general than they were.
- The technical explanation should connect combinatorial explosion to domain
  knowledge: Lighthill's most durable critique is that useful programs depended
  on detailed problem-specific knowledge rather than domain-general intelligence.
- Michie's DEC System 10/PDP-10 argument is the chapter's hardware/software
  dependency scene. The point is not only architecture; it is access to the
  accumulating US AI-oriented software and applications ecosystem.
- British-machine framing matters: Michie names ICL 4130 work at Edinburgh and
  argues that British systems needed LISP, POP-2, SNOBOL, QA4, PLANNER,
  CONNIVER, and related facilities if UK AI workers were to compete.

## Prose Guardrails

- Do not let "devastation" mean extinction. The system moved toward narrower,
  application-linked, and differently labeled research.
- Do not make the chapter purely social. The policy effect depends on a
  real technical critique: scaling, search, language, and problem-domain
  knowledge.
- Do not make the chapter purely technical. The same claims mattered because
  SRC controlled scarce machines, grants, and institutional legitimacy.
