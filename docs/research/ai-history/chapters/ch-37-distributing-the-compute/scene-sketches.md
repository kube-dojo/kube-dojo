# Scene Sketches: Chapter 37

## Scene 1: The Dead Drive
- **Action:** An engineer tries to process 10 terabytes of data, but the script crashes 90% of the way through because a single hard drive in the cluster failed.

## Scene 2: The Elegant Abstraction
- **Action:** Pedagogical explanation of MapReduce. Splitting a massive word-counting task across 1,000 workers, and seamlessly re-assigning work if a worker dies.

## Scene 3: Open Sourcing the Cluster
- **Action:** Yahoo and Doug Cutting build Hadoop, ensuring that big data infrastructure isn't just a Google monopoly.
