## Conflict Notes
- **Why did Symbolics fail?** Some attribute the fall of LISP machines strictly to bad business practices. The chapter will align with the epic's infrastructural thesis: they were outpaced by the general-purpose compute infrastructure of Moore's Law (PCs/Unix workstations), proving that custom symbolic hardware was an evolutionary dead end.
