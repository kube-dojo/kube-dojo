## Characterization Guardrails

- Do not portray Jelinek as anti-language. The anchored story is probabilistic engineering under uncertainty.
- Do not write a hero-only chapter. The evidence supports a lab/community story: IBM, Bell Labs, CMU, DARPA evaluations, and ACL/CL statistical NLP.
- Do not infer private motives, lab drama, or personality color from technical papers.
