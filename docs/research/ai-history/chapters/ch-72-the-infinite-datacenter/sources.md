## Conflict Notes

- Ch72 is about buildout, siting, financing, cooling, and local infrastructure.
  Ch70 owns power generation, PPAs, nuclear, and grid policy. Mention power
  only when it affects site selection or construction.
- Company datacenter announcements are not neutral audits. Attribute jobs,
  economic impact, savings, clean-energy claims, and community benefits.
- Planned gigawatts are not operational gigawatts. Use "planned," "under
  development," "can scale," "expected," and "in use" exactly.
- Water terminology must stay precise: withdrawal, discharge, consumption,
  replenishment, WUE, and closed-loop cooling are not interchangeable.
- Do not turn the closing chapter into a sustainability sermon. The stronger
  historical claim is that AI became an industrial-capacity problem.
- Wikipedia was useful for discovery only. It cannot be cited as evidence.
