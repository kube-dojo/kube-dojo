**Make cloud spending a first-class engineering concern.**

FinOps is the practice of bringing financial accountability to cloud spending. It's not about spending less — it's about spending wisely. Every dollar saved on idle resources is a dollar that can fund new features, better infrastructure, or higher salaries.
